About Us

Built in the Triangle. Powered by Our Own Tech.

Game Night Guild is a Hoeberlein Technologies experience — an interactive entertainment brand built from the ground up in the Triangle, North Carolina. Every event runs on proprietary software developed in-house by the same team that builds custom tech for local businesses.

This isn't a franchise. It's not a quiz app on a laptop. It's a full-production game night experience designed to drive foot traffic, build community, and give people a reason to come back next week.

We're actively booking venues across the Triangle region — from downtown Raleigh bars to neighborhood clubhouses across the Triangle. Let's build something your crowd won't stop talking about.
